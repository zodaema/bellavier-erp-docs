═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
UI SPECIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Colors:
──────
  Success (Start/Complete): #28a745 (green)
  Primary (Progress):       #845adf (purple)
  Warning (QC/Hold):        #ffc107 (yellow)
  Danger (Fail):            #dc3545 (red)
  Info (Resume):            #17a2b8 (cyan)
  Secondary:                #6c757d (gray)

Button Sizes:
─────────────
  Quick Mode Buttons:   60px height, 18px font
  Detail Mode Buttons:  40px height, 16px font
  Icon Size:            24px (Quick), 20px (Detail)

Spacing:
────────
  Card Padding:         16px (mobile), 24px (desktop)
  Button Gaps:          8px (Quick), 12px (Detail)
  Form Field Gaps:      16px

Typography:
───────────
  Page Title:           32px, bold
  Card Title:           20px, semibold
  Button Text:          18px (Quick), 16px (Detail)
  Form Labels:          14px
  Helper Text:          12px

Responsive Breakpoints:
───────────────────────
  Mobile:    < 768px   (Stack, full width)
  Tablet:    768-1024px (Grid 2 columns for buttons)
  Desktop:   > 1024px  (Split layout: Scan | Form)
